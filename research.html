How IoT Works?

A complete IoT system integrates four distinct components: sensors/devices, connectivity, data processing, and a user interface.

Here is how IoT works in a nuttshell; First, sensors or devices collect data from their environment. This could be as simple as a temperature reading or as complex as a full video feed. Then, that data is sent to the cloud. Once the data gets to the cloud, software performs some kind of processing on it. Finally, the information is made useful to the end-user in some way.

How big is IoT?
